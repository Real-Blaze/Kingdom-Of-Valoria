Welcome to the Kingdom of Valoria

The Kingdom of Valoria stands as a testament to friendship, creativity, and shared vision. Founded through the unity of great minds and noble hearts, our realm represents the power of collaboration and the beauty of diverse leadership working toward common goals.

Founded: February 7th, 2025 - The Great Threnidox (Founding Day)

Born from the vision of three Great Lords and expanded through the wisdom of our complete council, Valoria represents a new chapter in micronational excellence. Our founding marks the beginning of the Era Valoriana - the Valorian Age.

The Royal Council - Consilium Regalis

King Callistus

Sovereign Ruler of Valoria • Rex Supremus

His Royal Majesty King Callistus leads the Kingdom of Valoria with wisdom and vision. As the founding monarch, he guides the realm toward prosperity and unity among all tribes and territories. Bearer of the Sacred Valdroscepter (Crown Scepter).

Field Marshal Kaldor

Lord of War • Dominus Bellicus • Founder of Modesty State • Voidfather • Lord of Land Warfare

A founding Great Lord, Field Marshal Kaldor commands the military might of Valoria and serves as the Voidfather of Modesty, bringing strategic brilliance to the realm's defense. Master of the ancient Valdris Tacticus (War Strategy).

Father Zephyrus

Leader of Valorian Church • Sanctus Zephyrus • Air Chief Marshal • Lord of Curses & Blessings

The spiritual and aerial commander of Valoria, Father Zephyrus guides both the souls and skies of the kingdom with divine wisdom. Keeper of the Liber Benedictionis (Book of Blessings) and commander of the sky forces.

Prime Minister Aldric

Chief Minister of Valoria • Primus Ministrus

Prime Minister Aldric oversees the day-to-day governance of the kingdom, ensuring that the will of the crown is carried out with efficiency and justice. Guardian of the Codex Administratus (Administrative Code).

Lord Elion

Astrarch of Modesty • Astrarch Modestiae

As the Astrarch (state ruler) of Modesty, Lord Elion governs one of Valoria's most important territories with honor and dedication to the crown. Protector of the Sanctuarium Modestiae (Sanctuary of Modesty).

The Royal Laws of Valoria - Lex Valoriana

"These sacred laws, blessed by Father Zephyrus and decreed by His Majesty King Callistus, shall guide all citizens of Valoria in their daily Valdrix (noble living)."

Law I: Thou shalt not consume any Nutritum (food) without first performing the sacred ritual of Aqua Purification (hand washing). The penalty for violation is one hour of dishwashing duty.
Law II: All citizens must greet each other with the traditional Valorian phrase "Valdrax Zephyros!" (May the winds favor you!) at least once per day, lest they be required to compose a haiku about friendship.
Law III: No citizen may claim to be "bored" without first attempting to learn three new Vocabula Valoriana (Valorian words). Violation results in mandatory storytelling duty for the kingdom.
Law IV: The sacred Tempus Snackicus (snack time) must be respected by all. Interrupting someone's snack break is punishable by having to share your own snacks.
Law V: All disputes shall be settled through the ancient art of Gladius Papyrus (rock, paper, scissors), best of three rounds. The loser must compliment the winner's strategic prowess.
Law VI: Every citizen must contribute to the Royal Collection of Ridicula Facta (funny facts) at least once per month. Failure to do so results in temporary banishment to the Corner of Contemplation (for exactly 5 minutes).
Law VII: The wearing of mismatched socks is not only permitted but encouraged on Dies Sockicus (Sock Freedom Day), which occurs whenever someone declares it to be so.

The Six Noble Tribes - Tribus Nobilis

6

Our kingdom is built upon the foundation of six noble tribes, each with their own lord and traditions. Together, they form the diverse tapestry that makes Valoria strong and prosperous.

Each tribe contributes its unique strengths to the greater glory of the kingdom, following the ancient principle of Diversitas in Unitas (Diversity in Unity).

Tribal Motto: "Sex Tribus, Unum Regnum!"
(Six Tribes, One Kingdom!)

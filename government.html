Government Structure of the Kingdom of Valoria

Overview

The Kingdom of Valoria is governed by a dynamic structure that ensures representation and accountability at every level.

Branches of Government

  • Executive Branch: Led by the King, responsible for implementing laws and overseeing government operations.
  • Legislative Branch: Composed of the Royal Assembly, responsible for creating laws and representing the citizens.
  • Judicial Branch: Ensures laws are applied fairly and resolves disputes through the court system.

Local Governance

Valoria is divided into several regions, each governed by local councils that make decisions pertinent to their communities.
